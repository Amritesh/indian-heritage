The Silk Road Influence

By the 1st Century CE, the Kushan Empire controlled the crucial crossroads between China and the Roman Empire. This fusion created the 'Gandhara Style', where Greco-Roman techniques met Indian Buddhist iconography—a hallmark of early heritage.
