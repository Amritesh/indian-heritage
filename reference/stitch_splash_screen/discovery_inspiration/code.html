Legacy Chronicles

The Lost Art of
Bidriware Inlay

Originating in the 14th century under the Bahmani Sultans, Bidriware is a unique metal handicraft from Bidar. The blackened alloy of zinc and copper, inlaid with thin sheets of pure silver, was once considered the ultimate symbol of Deccani nobility. Legend says the soil of Bidar fort, used in the oxidizing process, contains unique chemicals found nowhere else on earth.
